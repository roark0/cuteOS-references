      |     0 OR 1         0 OR 1           0 OR 1         0 OR 1     |

      |                        NUMBER OF BYTES                        |
      +---------------------------------------------------------------+

      +---------------------------------------------------------------+
      |  OPCODE  |  MODR/M   |  SIB  |   DISPLACEMENT   |  IMMEDIATE  |
      |          |           |       |                  |             |
      |---------------------------------------------------------------|
      |  1 OR 2     0 OR 1    0 OR 1      0,1,2 OR 4       0,1,2 OR 4 |
      |- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -|
      |                        NUMBER OF BYTES                        |
      +---------------------------------------------------------------+
